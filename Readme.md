Privacy Policy for CamScanner India
Last Updated: May 13, 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how CamScanner India ("we," "our," or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience while using our document scanning services.
1. Information Collection and Use
A. Document Data (Scanning & Storage)
CamScanner India is designed with a privacy-first approach.
Local Processing: All document scanning, edge detection, and image filtering are performed locally on your device using the Google ML Kit Document Scanner API.
No Cloud Storage: We do not upload, store, or monitor the contents of your scanned documents on any external servers. Your documents remain entirely under your control on your device's local storage.
User-Initiated Deletion: The App allows you to select and delete documents from your dashboard. This action is permanent and occurs locally on your device. We do not maintain backups or "trash folders" of your deleted documents.
B. Device Permissions
To provide its services, the App requires the following permissions:
Camera: To capture images of the documents you wish to scan.
Storage/Media: To save generated PDFs to your device and to allow importing existing images from your gallery for scanning.
Internet: Used solely for the delivery of advertisements via Google AdMob and for the initial download of ML models by Google Play Services.
2. Sharing Functionality
When you use the "Share" feature (including multi-file sharing), you are explicitly choosing to send your document data to another application of your choice (e.g., WhatsApp, Gmail, or cloud storage providers). Once the data is shared, it is governed by the privacy policy of the receiving third-party application.
3. Third-Party Services
The App utilizes the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:
Google Play Services: Used for the core scanning engine. Google Privacy Policy
AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your advertising ID to personalize ads and analyze traffic. How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services
4. Data Security & Retention
Local Security: Since your documents are stored locally, their security depends on the security of your mobile device. We recommend using device-level encryption and passcodes.
App Uninstallation: Please note that uninstalling the App will result in the permanent loss of all scanned documents stored in the App's private folders unless you have manually saved them to a public folder (like "Downloads") using the "Save" feature.
5. Children’s Privacy
Our App does not specifically target or knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. We do not maintain any user profiles or databases.
6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon being posted.
