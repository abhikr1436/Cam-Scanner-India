Privacy Policy for CamScanner India
Last Updated: October 26, 2023
Abhijeet Kumar ("we," "our," or "us") operates the CamScanner India mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.
1. Information Collection and Use
We do not collect any personal identification information from our users. Your privacy is our priority.
2. Permissions and Data Processing
To provide our document scanning and PDF utility features, the app requires the following permissions:
•
Camera: Used solely for scanning documents and QR/Barcodes. Images are processed on-device using Google ML Kit and are not uploaded to any external servers.
•
Storage: Used to save the generated PDF documents and to allow you to import images or PDFs for merging/compression.
•
Internet: Used to display advertisements and to allow you to open external links (e.g., UPI payments, web links).
3. On-Device Processing
All document scanning, OCR (text recognition), and PDF generation happen locally on your device. Your scanned documents never leave your phone unless you explicitly choose to share them via another app (e.g., Email, WhatsApp).
4. Third-Party Service Providers
We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:
•
Google Play Services / ML Kit: Used for document and barcode scanning.
•
Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers and usage data to personalize ads and analyze performance. You can view their privacy policy here: Google Privacy & Terms.
5. Data Security
We value your trust in providing us your information. Since all processing is on-device, your documents are as secure as your phone is. We do not store your files on any cloud servers.
6. Children’s Privacy
Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
